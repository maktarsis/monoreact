This document intended to inform about the compatibility of monoreact with root external dependencies

#### from **0.31.0** (latest):

- react@**17.x.x**
- react-scripts@**5.x.x** (_will be cut in the future_)
- typescript@**4.x.x**

#### from **0.25.0** to **0.30.x**:

- react@**17.x.x**
- react-scripts@**4.x.x**
- typescript@**4.x.x**

#### from **0.1.0**  to **0.24.x**:

- react@**16.x.x**
- react-scripts@**3.x.x**
- typescript@**3.x.x**
